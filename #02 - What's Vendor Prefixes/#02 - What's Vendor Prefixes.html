Vendor Prefixes

Thes prefixes Used Before New Properites To Help old browsers Know them.

There are 4 Vendor Prefixes:

For Ex We Will Use Thes Perfixes With box-shadow; properity

-webkit-box-shadow; (Chrome, Safari, newer versions of Opera, almost all iOS browsers (including Firefox for iOS); basically, any WebKit based browser)

-moz-box-shadow; (Firefox)

-o-box-shadow; (Old, pre-WebKit, versions of Opera)

-ms-box-shadow; (Internet Explorer and Microsoft Edge)
